# CatalystIQ Demo Script
**Target duration: 3-4 minutes | Record in Loom, OBS, or Teams**

---

## Pre-Recording Setup

- [ ] Open Microsoft 365 Copilot (Teams or microsoft365.com)
- [ ] Have CatalystIQ published and available as an extension
- [ ] Open a second monitor with this script for reference
- [ ] Use a clean browser profile (no distracting notifications)
- [ ] Record at 1920×1080, 30fps
- [ ] Speak clearly, slightly slower than normal pace

---

## Scene 1 — Hook (0:00 - 0:20)

**SHOW:** A split screen: one side shows a frustrated HR manager drowning in spreadsheets, the other shows a blank "Where do we start with AI?" whiteboard.

Or simply record yourself saying:

> "70% of enterprise AI initiatives fail — not because of bad technology, but because organizations don't know where they stand, don't have a plan, and don't know what their people need to learn. CatalystIQ solves all three — in a 5-minute conversation."

---

## Scene 2 — Open the Agent (0:20 - 0:35)

**SHOW:** Open Microsoft 365 Copilot. Navigate to Copilot extensions. Select **CatalystIQ**.

**SAY:**
> "CatalystIQ lives right inside Microsoft 365 Copilot — no new app to install, no new login. Employees find it where they already work."

---

## Scene 3 — Welcome Flow (0:35 - 1:00)

**SHOW:** Type "Hello" and watch the welcome message appear.

**SAY (as you type):**
> "Let's see it in action. I'll say hello..."

**SHOW the bot response:** Welcome message with the 4 benefits listed.

**TYPE:** "Praveen, Software Engineer"

**SHOW:** Role selection options appear.

**SELECT:** "Software Engineer / Developer" then "Engineering / Technology" then "Intermediate"

**SAY:**
> "The agent collects just enough context to personalize everything — role, department, experience level. No forms, no surveys. Just a conversation."

---

## Scene 4 — Adaptive Assessment (1:00 - 1:50)

**SHOW:** Questions 1-3 appearing and the user answering.

**TYPE responses:**
- Q1: "B — We use AI tools but they're not standardized"
- Q2: "C — A few people are learning but most haven't started"
- Q3: "C — We occasionally use AI for one-off tasks"
- Q4: "C — We're aware we need policies but haven't created them"
- Q5 (Engineering): "B — I use GitHub Copilot for code generation regularly"
- Q6: "B — We've identified some use cases but haven't validated them"
- Q7: "C — We've heard about RAI but don't actively apply it"

**SAY (during questions):**
> "Seven adaptive questions. The fifth question changes based on your role — engineers get a technical AI question, executives get a strategy question. The agent is thinking about who it's talking to."

**SHOW:** The score card appears with dimension scores.

**SAY:**
> "And here's the result — an AI Maturity Scorecard across four dimensions. Ours shows we're Building Momentum — 43% — with Technology and Governance as our biggest gaps."

---

## Scene 5 — Work IQ Gap Analysis (1:50 - 2:20)

**SHOW:** "Searching our AI transformation knowledge base..." appears, followed by the 3 gaps with cited recommendations.

**SAY:**
> "Here's where Work IQ comes in. CatalystIQ doesn't give generic advice — it searches your organization's SharePoint knowledge base and surfaces specific, grounded recommendations. Notice it cites the source document — so employees know this advice comes from your company's approved policies, not the internet."

**HIGHLIGHT:** The citation badges showing document sources.

**SAY:**
> "Top 3 gaps — Technology, Governance, People — each with concrete next steps, each backed by our knowledge base."

---

## Scene 6 — Roadmap Generation (2:20 - 2:50)

**SHOW:** Click "Yes, generate my roadmap!"

**SAY:**
> "One click..."

**SHOW:** The 30/60/90 day roadmap appears — month by month, tailored to the identified gaps.

**SAY:**
> "A personalized 30/60/90 day transformation roadmap. Not generic advice — specific actions for a Software Engineer with Technology and Governance gaps. Week 1, Week 2, Day 60, Day 90. Real checkboxes."

**TYPE:** "Yes, send it!"

**SHOW:** Confirmation that email was sent and Planner tasks were created.

**SAY:**
> "Behind the scenes, Power Automate emails the roadmap and creates tasks in Microsoft Planner — automatically. The employee's manager can see those tasks. No manual follow-up needed."

---

## Scene 7 — Responsible AI Audit (2:50 - 3:15)

**SHOW:** Type "Can you run a Responsible AI audit on our new job screening tool?"

**SHOW:** Topic 7 starts — RAI principles walkthrough.

**SAY:**
> "CatalystIQ isn't just about upskilling. It's a full governance companion. Ask it to audit any AI use case, and it walks your team through Microsoft's 6 Responsible AI principles — Fairness, Reliability, Privacy, Inclusiveness, Transparency, Accountability — and flags concerns before deployment. It escalates to your AI Center of Excellence if it finds serious issues."

---

## Scene 8 — The Org-Level View (3:15 - 3:35)

**SHOW:** Switch to Power BI. Open the CatalystIQ Analytics dashboard showing:
- Organizational AI Maturity heatmap (by department)
- Average scores trending up over 30 days
- Top 3 gaps across the organization

**SAY:**
> "And leadership gets this — an organizational intelligence dashboard. Not just one person's journey, but the entire company's AI readiness, in real time. HR sees which teams need support. The CIO sees where to invest. The AI CoE sees which policies to prioritize. CatalystIQ turns individual conversations into organizational intelligence."

---

## Scene 9 — Closing (3:35 - 4:00)

**SHOW:** Return to the Copilot chat. CatalystIQ visible.

**SAY:**
> "CatalystIQ lives in Microsoft 365 Copilot, built in Copilot Studio, powered by Work IQ, connected to your SharePoint, Planner, and Outlook via Power Automate. No new tools. No new logins. Just AI transformation — where your employees already work."

> "I'm Praveen from Prodapt. This is CatalystIQ — because AI transformation starts with knowing where you are."

**END SCREEN:** Show the GitHub repo URL and project name.

---

## Recording Tips

- **Don't rush** — judges watch demos at 1x speed and miss fast interactions
- **Zoom in** on key moments (the scorecard, the citations, the Planner tasks)
- **Narrate what you're doing** before you do it — let the audience anticipate
- **Show the citations** — this is the Work IQ evidence judges need to see
- **Smile** — energy is contagious even in a video
- **Edit out mistakes** — don't include fumbled navigation or loading screens
